What is Zoho Desk?

Zoho Desk is a cloud-based help desk software designed to streamline customer service operations. It helps businesses manage support tickets, automate tasks, and deliver better customer experiences through multiple communication channels.

Key Features

  • Multi-channel ticketing (email, chat, social)
  • Workflow automation and SLA rules
  • Customizable Help Center and Knowledge Base
  • AI Assistant (Zia) for smart responses
  • Integrations with CRM, Projects, and more
